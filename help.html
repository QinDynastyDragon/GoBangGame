Rules

Go Bang (Chinese: "五子棋"，"五目", etc.) is a traditional competitive chess game played by two players.

Each player take turn in placing stones on the board (stones are placed on the intesection points of the grid lines). The goal of each player is to form a "5-in-a-row" with the player's stones.
